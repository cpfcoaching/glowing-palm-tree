# Newsletter Ideation

## Overview

A systematic approach to generating fresh angles for newsletter topics using proven design thinking frameworks.

## When to Use This Skill

- Brainstorming new article topics.
- When a topic feels "over-done" or generic.
- Developing multiple pieces of content from one broad theme.

## Systematic Frameworks

- **SCAMPER**: Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, Reverse.
- **Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD)**: What is the reader actually trying to achieve?
- **Contrarian Angle**: Challenge the consensus or a hidden assumption.
- **Time-Horizon Shifts**: Look at the topic from a 10-year past or 50-year future perspective.
- **Stakeholder Rotation**: View the topic through the eyes of various roles (CEO, Dev, User).

## Output Requirements

Generate 5-7 unique angles including:

- **Headline**: Compelling and specific.
- **Framework**: Which framework was used to find this angle.
- **Target Audience**: Who specifically is this for?
- **Value Proposition**: Why should they read it?

## Anti-Patterns

❌ **Obvious Takes**: Avoid the most common or first-thought angle.
