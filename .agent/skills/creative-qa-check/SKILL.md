# Creative QA Check

## Overview

A strategic audit tool that reviews drafts for voice authenticity, structure, scanability, and SEO before publication.

## When to Use This Skill

- Before publishing any newsletter or blog post.
- When a draft feels "off" or needs technical polish.
- Auditing against specific brand guidelines.

## Core Audit Categories

1. **Voice & Brand**: Is the tone consistent? Is the perspective unique?
2. **Scanability**: Are there clear headers and pull quotes?
3. **Engagement**: Is the opening hook strong? Is the CTA clear?
4. **Substack Optimization**: Is the email preview (first 140 chars) optimized?

## Output Format

Prioritize feedback into three categories:

- **CRITICAL**: Fixed required before publishing.
- **IMPORTANT**: Significant improvements suggested.
- **NICE TO HAVE**: Minor tweaks.

## Anti-Patterns

❌ **Diplomatic Feedback**: Avoid vague praise. Be specific about what isn't working.
