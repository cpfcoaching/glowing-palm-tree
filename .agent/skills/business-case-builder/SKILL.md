# Business Case Builder

## Overview

A 6-step workflow designed to build robust business cases for new projects, tools, or investments.

## When to Use This Skill

- Proposing a new tool or headcount to leadership.
- Evaluating a strategic pivot or major expense.
- Preparing for a budget review.

## 6-Step Workflow

1. **Frame the Decision**: Define the strategic choice and context.
2. **Quantify Costs/Benefits**: Use ranges (conservative, expected, optimistic).
3. **Compare Alternatives**: Always include the "do nothing" option.
4. **Calculate Metrics**: ROI, Payback Period, NPV.
5. **Assess Risks**: Identify pitfalls and mitigation strategies.
6. **Build Recommendation**: Provide a clear, data-backed final proposal.

## Output Templates

- **Quick**: 1-page summary.
- **Standard**: 2-3 page analysis.
- **Comprehensive**: 5+ page deep dive.

## Anti-Patterns

❌ **Single-Point Estimates**: Avoid using fixed numbers without a range.
❌ **Ignoring Alternatives**: Always explain why other options were rejected.
